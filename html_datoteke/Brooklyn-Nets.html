    Brooklyn Nets on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1967, the Nets in its history, had 2 NBA Finals appearances but are yet to win an NBA title. The team's most recent NBA Finals appearance was 20 years ago when they were defeated by the Tim Duncan-led San Antonio Spurs back in 2003.

    On NBA 2K24, Mikal Bridges is Brooklyn Nets' highest rated player, followed by Nicolas Claxton in second place, and Cameron Johnson in third.
