    Oklahoma City Thunder on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1967, the Thunder in its history, had won 1 title out of 4 NBA Finals appearances. The team's only title was won 44 years ago when it defeated the Washington Bullets led by Dennis Johnson back in 1979.

    On NBA 2K24, Shai Gilgeous-Alexander is Oklahoma City Thunder's highest rated player, followed by Josh Giddey in second place, and Jalen Williams in third.
