    Cleveland Cavaliers on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1970, the Cavaliers in its history, had won 1 title out of 5 NBA Finals appearances. The team's only title was won 7 years ago when it defeated the Golden State Warriors with the famous 3-1 comeback back in 2016.

    On NBA 2K24, Donovan Mitchell is Cleveland Cavaliers' highest rated player, followed by Darius Garland in second place, and Evan Mobley in third.
