    Atlanta Hawks on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1946, the Hawks in its history, had won 1 title out of 4 NBA Finals appearances. The team's only title was won 65 years ago when it defeated the Boston Celtics lead by Bob Pettit back in 1958.

    On NBA 2K24, Trae Young is Atlanta Hawks' highest rated player, followed by Dejounte Murray in second place, and Clint Capela in third.
