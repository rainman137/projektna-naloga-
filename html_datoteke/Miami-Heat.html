    Miami Heat on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1988, the Heat in its history, had won 3 titles out of 7 NBA Finals appearances. The team's most recent title was won 10 years ago when it defeated the San Antonio Spurs led by LeBron James back in 2013.

    On NBA 2K24, Jimmy Butler is Miami Heat's highest rated player, followed by Bam Adebayo in second place, and Tyler Herro in third.
