    Orlando Magic on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1989, the Magic in its history, had 2 NBA Finals appearances but are yet to win an NBA title. The team's most recent NBA Finals appearance was 14 years ago when they were defeated by the Kobe Bryant-led Los Angeles Lakers back in 2009.

    On NBA 2K24, Paolo Banchero is Orlando Magic's highest rated player, followed by Franz Wagner in second place, and Wendell Carter Jr. in third.
