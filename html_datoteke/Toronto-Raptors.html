    Founded in 1995, the Raptors in its history, had won 1 title out of 1 NBA Finals appearance. The team's only title was won 4 years ago when it defeated the Golden State Warriors led by Kawhi Leonard back in 2019.

    On NBA 2K24, Pascal Siakam is Toronto Raptors' highest rated player, followed by Scottie Barnes in second place, and OG Anunoby in third.
