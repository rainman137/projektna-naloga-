    Milwaukee Bucks on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1968, the Bucks in its history, had won 2 titles out of 3 NBA Finals appearances. The team's most recent title was won 2 years ago when it defeated the Phoenix Suns led by Giannis Antetokounmpo back in 2021.

    On NBA 2K24, Giannis Antetokounmpo is Milwaukee Bucks' highest rated player, followed by Damian Lillard in second place, and Khris Middleton in third.
