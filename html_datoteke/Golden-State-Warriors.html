    Golden State Warriors on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1946, the Warriors in its history, had won 7 titles out of 12 NBA Finals appearances. The team's most recent title was won 1 year ago when it defeated the Boston Celtics 4-2 led by the Finals MVP Steph Curry back in 2022.

    On NBA 2K24, Stephen Curry is Golden State Warriors' highest rated player, followed by Chris Paul in second place, and Klay Thompson in third.
