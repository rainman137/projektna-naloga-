    Utah Jazz on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1974, the Jazz in its history, had 2 NBA Finals appearances but are yet to win an NBA title. The team's most recent NBA Finals appearance was 25 years ago when they were defeated by the Michael Jordan-led Chicago Bulls back in 1998.

    On NBA 2K24, Lauri Markkanen is Utah Jazz's highest rated player, followed by Walker Kessler in second place, and John Collins in third.
