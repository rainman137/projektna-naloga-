    Houston Rockets on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1967, the Rockets in its history, had won 2 titles out of 4 NBA Finals appearances. The team's most recent title was won 28 years ago when it defeated the Orlando Magic led by Hakeem Olajuwon back in 1995.

    On NBA 2K24, Jalen Green is Houston Rockets' highest rated player, followed by Fred VanVleet in second place, and Alperen Sengun in third.
