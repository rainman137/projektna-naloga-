    Boston Celtics on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1946, the Celtics in its history, had won 17 titles out of 22 NBA Finals appearances. The team's most recent title was won 15 years ago when it defeated the Los Angeles Lakers powered by its "Big 4" (Kevin Garnett, Paul Pierce, Ray Allen, and Rajon Rondo) back in 2008.

    On NBA 2K24, Jayson Tatum is Boston Celtics' highest rated player, followed by Jaylen Brown in second place, and Jrue Holiday in third.
