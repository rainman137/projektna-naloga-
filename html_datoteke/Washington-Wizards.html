    Washington Wizards on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1961, the Wizards in its history, had won 1 title out of 4 NBA Finals appearances. The team's only title was won 45 years ago when it defeated the Seattle SuperSonics led by Wes Unseld back in 1978.

    On NBA 2K24, Kyle Kuzma is Washington Wizards' highest rated player, followed by Jordan Poole in second place, and Tyus Jones in third.
