    Portland Trail Blazers on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1970, the Trail Blazers in its history, had won 1 title out of 3 NBA Finals appearances. The team's only title was won 46 years ago when it defeated the Philadelphia 76ers led by Bill Walton back in 1977.

    On NBA 2K24, Deandre Ayton is Portland Trail Blazers' highest rated player, followed by Anfernee Simons in second place, and Malcolm Brogdon in third.
