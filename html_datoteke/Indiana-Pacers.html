    Indiana Pacers on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1967, the Pacers in its history, had 1 NBA Finals appearance but are yet to win an NBA title. The team's most recent NBA Finals appearance was 23 years ago when they were defeated by the Shaq-led Los Angeles Lakers back in 2000.

    On NBA 2K24, Tyrese Haliburton is Indiana Pacers' highest rated player, followed by Myles Turner in second place, and Bennedict Mathurin in third.
