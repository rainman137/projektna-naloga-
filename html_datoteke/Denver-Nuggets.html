    Denver Nuggets on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1967, the Denver Nuggets have won their first NBA title after beating Miami Heat in Game 5 of the 2023 NBA Finals. Nikola Jokic finished the 2023 NBA Finals averaging 30.2 points, 14 rebounds, 7.2 assists, and 1.4 steals and was named NBA Finals MVP.

    On NBA 2K24, Nikola Jokic is Denver Nuggets' highest rated player, followed by Jamal Murray in second place, and Aaron Gordon in third.
