    Phoenix Suns on NBA 2K24

    Founded in 1968, the Suns in its history, had 3 NBA Finals appearances but are yet to win an NBA title. The team's most recent championship series appearance was in the 2021 NBA Finals when they were defeated by the Giannis Antetokounmpo-led Milwaukee Bucks.

    On NBA 2K24, Kevin Durant is Phoenix Suns' highest rated player, followed by Devin Booker in second place, and Bradley Beal in third.
